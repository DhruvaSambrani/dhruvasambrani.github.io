Relativistic Black Body Radiation

 JS Bagla   | Winter 2018

As part of my First Year Winter Project, I took up the project Black body radiation in special relativistic frames under JS Bagla, IISER Mohali, where I looked at how Black Body Radiation transforms in a frame that is moving uniformly with respect to the source. We see that the radiation is no more uniform, leading to a force on the particle. The quantitative analysis of this force is done using numerical methods in Julia.

Introduction to Quantum Mechanics and Computing

  Arvind    | Summer 2019

My First Year Summer Project was an introductory reading to the Mathematical Structures that underlie Quantum Mechanics and then take it forward to a theoretical introduction to Quantum Information and Quantum Computing. I also attempt to simulate a Quantum Computer on a Classical computer and making a Julia module for the same. This paper serves as a very brief introduction to Quantum Computing and documentation of the code in the form while publishing.
